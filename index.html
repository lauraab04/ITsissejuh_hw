Cat Care 101: A Guide for New Cat Owners

Feed your cat healthy food. Choose suitable food dishes and water bowls. Have a litter box (and accessories) ready to go. Brush your cat regularly. Bathe your cat (but not too often). Keep your cat's claws trimmed. Enjoy playtime! Invest in a scratching post or scratcher. Give your cat a place to nap. Have treats on hand. Purchase a comfortable cat carrier. Brush your cat's teeth. Find a good veterinarian. Teach children how to treat your cat. Make your home cat-safe. Keep your cat indoors.
